Ravioli

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped spinach (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups marinara sauce (for serving)
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)

Time: 40 mins

Serves: 4

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the eggs and olive oil.
  2. Mix the eggs and oil into the flour until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
  3. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  4. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, chopped spinach (if using), nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Divide the dough into 4 pieces. Roll out each piece on a floured surface until very thin.
  6. Place small spoonfuls of the filling mixture onto one sheet of dough, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  7. Brush around the filling with water, then place another sheet of dough on top. Press around the filling to seal, making sure to remove any air pockets.
  8. Cut out the ravioli using a knife or ravioli cutter. Press the edges to ensure they are sealed.
  9. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the ravioli and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until they float to the top.
