Greek Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Time: 15 mins

Serves: 2

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken breasts to the bowl and toss to coat them in the marinade. Let them marinate for at least 15 minutes.
  3. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Grill the chicken breasts for 6-8 minutes per side, or until they are cooked through and have nice grill marks.
  5. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes.
  6. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese, Kalamata olives, and chopped fresh parsley over the top of the chicken.
  7. Serve the Greek chicken with lemon wedges on the side.
