Meat and Chips

Ingredients

  • 500g beef steak, cut into strips
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large potatoes, cut into chips
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Ketchup or your favorite dipping sauce (for serving)

Time: 40 mins

Serves: 4

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced onion and cook until it becomes translucent.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add the beef strips to the skillet and cook until they are browned on all sides.
  5. Season with paprika, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  6. Reduce the heat to medium and let the beef cook until it is tender, about 10-15 minutes.
  7. Meanwhile, heat the vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 180°C (350°F).
  8. Fry the potato chips in batches until they are golden brown and crispy, about 5-7 minutes per batch.
  9. Remove the chips from the oil and drain on paper towels. Season with salt to taste.
  10. Serve the beef strips hot with the freshly fried chips.
  11. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and serve with ketchup or your favorite dipping sauce.
