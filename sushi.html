Sushi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 200g fresh salmon or tuna, sliced
  • Soy sauce, for serving
  • Pickled ginger, for serving
  • Wasabi, for serving

Time: 30 mins

Serves: 2

Steps

  1. Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Combine the rice and water in a rice cooker and cook according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  3. In a small saucepan, combine the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Cook over medium heat until the sugar and salt are dissolved. Let it cool.
  4. Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a large bowl and gently fold in the vinegar mixture. Let the rice cool to room temperature.
  5. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat, shiny side down.
  6. Wet your hands with water and spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving a 1-inch border at the top.
  7. Arrange the cucumber, avocado, and fish slices in a line along the bottom edge of the rice.
  8. Using the bamboo mat, roll the sushi tightly from the bottom, applying gentle pressure to shape the roll.
  9. Once rolled, use a sharp knife to slice the sushi roll into bite-sized pieces.
  10. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.
  11. Serve the sushi with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi.
